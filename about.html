"Hi Beloved"

The King's Vessels Annual Praise Blast

April 2022.

The Praise blast programme brings together spirit-filled gospel artist to lead and tune our our hearts through the songs of praises and worship. Through this event, alot of testimonies have been springing forth. We are trusting God that you too will get your testimony. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list to include popular Nigeria artist as well as world renown gospel singers.

Please contact us via Email for any further questions about Praise Blast 2022.

praiseblast.rccg@gmail.com

RCCGCOG King's Vessel organises her concert "Praise Blast 2022".

This event is held annually. This year promises to be a more exciting experience in God's presence. When praise goes up, blessing comes down. In the attitude of thanksgiving, worship and praise, a lot of life challenges are solved. The Lord is waiting for those that will worship and praise him in spirit and in truth. Come in total obedience, and expect the blessing of God upon your life.
